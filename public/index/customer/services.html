Mail and Shipping Services

We provide serveral delivery services with various prices, speed, and more. Most of our services provides automatic tracking.

Priority Mail Express

  • Price: Starts at $27.75

  • Delivery Speed: Next day to 2 days

  • Weight: Up to 70lbs

Priority Mail

  • Price:

    Mailing Box: From $9.20

Ground

  • Low rates for packages that only need to be delivered within 2-5 days. Good option for HAZMAT items that can't travel by air.

  • Price: From $4.25

  • Delivery Speed: 2-5 days

  • Weight: Up to 70lbs inside the US. Packages up to 15.999 oz is priced by ounces.

First Class

  • For sending letter envelops (below 3.5oz) or large envelop (below 13 oz) that can flatten and bend.

  • Price:
    Postcards: $0.41
    Letters: $0.56

  • Delivery Speed: 2-5 days
  • Weight: Single-piece envelops weighs up 10 3.5oz, and large envelops up to 13oz.

Media Class
